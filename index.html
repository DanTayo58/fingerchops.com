Our Full Story

Vision: To have our tasty and nutritious baked goods on every tables of every home in Nigeria.

Mission: To harness the power of food to connect people, cultures and create a better world for living.

Founded in the heart of Warri City, Delta State, Fingerchops Ventures started as a small family bakery with a big vision. We believed that every Nigerian family deserved access to freshly baked, preservative-free bread made with love and tradition.

Over the years, we've grown from that single oven to become a beloved bakery serving communities across Warri, Sapele, Asaba, Benin, Bayelsa, Port Harcourt, and Anambra States. Our commitment remains unchanged: quality ingredients, traditional methods, and bread that tastes like home.

This Is Us

The Heart of Fingerchops

We don't just bake bread to sell. We bake to nurture, empower, and lift our local Delta State community every single day.

Community Care

Loaves of Hope

Every week, we pack up crates of fresh, warm, nutritious Golden Butter bread and deliver them directly to local motherless baby homes, orphanages, and struggling family shelters in and around Warri. To us, feeding the community is a responsibility we carry with absolute pride.

Spreading warmth where it is needed most
Free Training

Bakers of Tomorrow

Through our free Vocational Bakery apprentice program, we've trained over 450 passionate youths across Warri, Sapele, and Delta State. We provide them with hands-on skills, mentorship, and professional baking tools for free so they can create sustainable livelihoods.

Over 450 local youths empowered with career skills
Our Devotion

While the rest of the city rests, our warm lights are already on. At exactly 2:00 AM, our devoted team of 45 local bakers begins preparing the pure dough by hand, ensuring that the warm aroma of fresh, healthy bread greets our neighbors right at sunrise.

Honest labor, baked fresh for every home
